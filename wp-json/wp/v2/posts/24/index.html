    This exercise will primarily work your front delts<\/a>, with your triceps <\/a>and side delts<\/a> as secondarily working muscles. In addition, you will use your core, hips, and rotator cuff as stabilizing musculature throughout the exercise.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
